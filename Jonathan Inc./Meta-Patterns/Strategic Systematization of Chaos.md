### Be Slightly Evil - V. Rao
**Meta-Pattern** The book explores what Rao calls "systematization of chaos" - entering disordered contexts, creating transferable systems, gaining influence through infrastructure-building, then moving on when work becomes maintenance-oriented. This is the fundamental pattern that drives both personal career cycles and broader institutional evolution through creative destruction.
