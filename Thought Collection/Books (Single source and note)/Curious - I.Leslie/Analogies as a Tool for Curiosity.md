##### Are analogies a tool for us to cultivate curiousity? (linked to D. Berlyne and the concept of knowledge effect and relationship to curiousity)

**Analogies are powerful tools for cultivating curiosity** because they leverage Berlyne's fundamental insight: they position us perfectly in the "knowledge zone" where we know enough to be intrigued but not enough to be satisfied, creating the productive tension that drives sustained intellectual exploration.
