#### Curiosity and Knowledge
Daniel Berlyne says curiosity is paradoxical as it is stimulated by understanding and the absence of understanding.

If we know nothing about a subject we have a hard time to engage. However the more we know about something the more intense our curiosity about something we don't know yet, especially if we feel it will be insightful.
