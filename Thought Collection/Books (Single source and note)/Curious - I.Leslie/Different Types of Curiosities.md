##### What are the definitions of the different type of curiousities outlined in the book?
- **Diverse curiousity** - The desire for novelty and new experiences - essentially our attraction to "what's new"
- **Epistemic curiousity** - The deeper, more disciplined desire for knowledge and understanding - our drive to truly comprehend
- **Empathic curiousity** - Curiosity about the thoughts, feelings, and perspectives of other people
