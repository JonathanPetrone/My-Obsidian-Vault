**"Questions are the best weapons of the weak against the strong"**

A lot of bad leadership comes from an inability or unwillingness to ask questions.
> ...The dumbest questions unlock conversations

In bureaucracies there is an incentive for senior managers to stop asking questions. Ignorance becomes power. Retention of power is an end goal.

In companies success can stop innovation, there forms a resistance to innovate in order to protect success.
