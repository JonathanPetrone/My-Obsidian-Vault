### Experts are Poor Questioners
Questioning often has an inverse relationship to expertise -- such that, within their subject areas, experts are apt to be poor questioners. 

If you "know" there is no reason to ask; yet if you don't ask, then you are relying on expert knowledge that is certainly limited, may be outdated and could be altogether wrong.

### The Feeling of Knowing
The feeling of knowing is only a feeling or a sensation. The feeling is so strong that people walk around convinced they have more answers than they actually do.

### Advantages of the Beginner Mind
"In the beginners mind there are many possibilities, but in the experts there are few". The mind of the beginner is free from the habits of the expert.
